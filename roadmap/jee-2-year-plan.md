# 🏆 Full 2-Year IIT JEE Preparation Plan

This is the **complete roadmap from Class 11 → JEE exam**.

---

## 📅 Phase-wise Plan

---

## 🧩 Phase 1: Foundation (Class 11)

Duration: 12 months

Focus:
- Concept clarity
- NCERT + basic problems
- Avoid backlogs

👉 Output:
- Strong fundamentals
- Problem-solving base

---

## ⚙️ Phase 2: Consolidation (Early Class 12)

Duration: 6 months

Focus:
- Complete Class 12 syllabus
- Start PYQs
- Revise Class 11

👉 Output:
- Full syllabus coverage

---

## 🚀 Phase 3: Mastery (Final 6 Months)

Focus:
- Mock tests
- Advanced problems
- Weak area improvement

👉 Output:
- Exam readiness

---

## 🗓️ Ideal Daily Schedule

| Time | Activity |
|------|--------|
| Morning | Concept learning |
| Afternoon | Problem solving |
| Evening | Revision |
| Night | PYQs / Mock analysis |

---

## 📊 Study Distribution

- 40% Concepts
- 40% Problem Solving
- 20% Revision

---

## 🧠 Revision Strategy

- Weekly revision
- Monthly full revision
- Formula notebook
- Error log book

---

## 🔥 Mock Test Strategy

- Start after 60-70% syllabus
- 1 test/week → then 2-3/week
- Analyze deeply

---

## ⚠️ Biggest Mistakes to Avoid

- Backlogs
- No revision
- Ignoring PYQs
- Not analyzing tests

---

## 🎯 Final 3 Months Strategy

- Only revision + mocks
- No new topics
- Focus on weak areas
- Improve speed & accuracy
