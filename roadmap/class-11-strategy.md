# 🚀 Class 11 IIT JEE Roadmap

Class 11 is the **foundation year**. If done right, JEE becomes 50% easier.

---

## 🎯 Goals for Class 11

- Build strong fundamentals
- Develop problem-solving skills
- Avoid backlog accumulation
- Master NCERT + JEE basics

---

## 📚 Subjects Strategy

### 🧮 Mathematics
Focus Topics:
- Sets, Relations, Functions
- Trigonometry
- Quadratic Equations
- Sequences & Series
- Straight Lines
- Limits & Derivatives (intro)

👉 Strategy:
- Understand concepts deeply
- Solve standard problems repeatedly
- Maintain formula notebook

---

### ⚡ Physics
Focus Topics:
- Units & Dimensions
- Kinematics
- Laws of Motion
- Work, Energy, Power
- Rotational Mechanics
- Gravitation

👉 Strategy:
- Visualize every concept
- Focus on derivations
- Solve numericals daily

---

## 🗓️ Weekly Study Plan

| Day | Focus |
|-----|------|
| Mon–Fri | Concepts + Practice |
| Sat | Revision + PYQs |
| Sun | Full syllabus test (small) |

---

## 🔁 Daily Routine (Ideal)

- 2 hours Physics
- 2 hours Maths
- 1 hour Revision
- 20–30 problems per subject

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Ignoring basics
- Not revising regularly
- Solving only easy problems
- Backlogs (BIGGEST mistake)

---

## 🧠 Milestones

- Month 3 → Comfortable with basics
- Month 6 → Intermediate problems
- Month 9 → PYQs start
- Month 12 → Strong foundation ready
