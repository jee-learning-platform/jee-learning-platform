# 🎯 Class 12 IIT JEE Roadmap

Class 12 is about **execution + mastery + exam readiness**.

---

## 🎯 Goals for Class 12

- Complete syllabus on time
- Strengthen Class 11 concepts
- Practice PYQs extensively
- Prepare for Boards + JEE together

---

## 📚 Subjects Strategy

### 🧮 Mathematics
Focus Topics:
- Continuity & Differentiability
- Applications of Derivatives
- Integration
- Differential Equations
- Vectors & 3D
- Probability

👉 Strategy:
- Practice mixed problems
- Focus on speed + accuracy
- Solve previous year questions

---

### ⚡ Physics
Focus Topics:
- Electrostatics
- Current Electricity
- Magnetism
- Optics
- Modern Physics

👉 Strategy:
- Focus on conceptual clarity
- Practice numericals rigorously
- Use diagrams + visualization

---

## 🔁 Daily Routine

- 2–3 hrs Physics

## 🔥 PYQ Strategy

- Start early (don’t wait till end)
- Solve topic-wise
- Analyze mistakes

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

- Ignoring Class 11 topics
- Over-focusing on Boards only
- Not taking mock tests
- Poor time management
